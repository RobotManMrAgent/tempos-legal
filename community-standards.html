Tempos Community Standards

Effective July 11, 2026

Tempos has zero tolerance for objectionable content or abusive behavior. These standards apply to posts, comments, profiles, and uploaded songs.

Prohibited content

Do not share content that is illegal, hateful, harassing, sexually explicit, exploitative, graphically violent, threatening, fraudulent, or invasive of another person's privacy.

Respect other people's rights

Share only video, audio, images, and writing that you created or have permission to use. Do not impersonate others or publish private information without consent. Copyright notices can be sent to jack.cockfield5@gmail.com.

Report and block

Use the report action on a post, comment, profile, or uploaded song when it may violate these standards. Use Block on a profile to hide that user's content and prevent unwanted interaction.

Enforcement

Tempos reviews reports and may remove content, limit features, suspend accounts, or terminate accounts based on severity, context, and repeated violations. You can permanently remove your own account from Profile → Settings → Delete Account.

Urgent safety concerns

If someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services. Tempos support is not an emergency service.
