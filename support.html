Tempos Support

Tempos records video takes to a song, cuts them to the beat, and lets you rearrange the clips and share the result.

Get help

Email jack.cockfield5@gmail.com and include what you were doing, what you expected, and what happened instead. Include your device model and iOS version when relevant, but never send a password or authentication code.

Report content or users

Use the in-app report action on any post, comment, profile, or uploaded song. Tempos reviews reports and may remove violating content or restrict accounts. You can block users from their profile to hide their content immediately.

Manage your account

  • Reset your password from the sign-in screen.
  • Delete your account in the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account.

Copyright notices

Email jack.cockfield5@gmail.com and identify the copyrighted work, the content you believe infringes it, where that content appears, your contact information, and a good-faith statement that the use is not authorized. Tempos may request more information before acting.
